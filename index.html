B.Sc Nursing Entrance Exam Made Mandatory from 2023-24

The Karnataka State Government made B.Sc Nursing entrance exam mandatory for admissions to the course from the academic year 2023-24.

Candidates interested in pursuing B.Sc Nursing in Karnataka have to appear in the Common Entrance Test (CET) conducted by the Karnataka Examinations Authority (KEA) to be eligible for B.Sc Nursing admissions in colleges across the state.

Important Note: There was no compulsory entrance exam for securing a B.Sc Nursing seat in Karnataka till 2022-23 and candidates were allotted seats based on marks secured in 10+2/ 2nd PUC exams. From 2023-24 onwards, Nursing aspirants have to mandatorily write KCET and attend the state counselling to secure a B.Sc Nursing seat at any of the best nursing colleges in Karnataka. KEA KCET 2025 B.Sc Nursing Counselling will be conducted for both government and management quota seats.

B.Sc Nursing Entrance Exam Syllabus & Eligibility

Let's take a look at some important details, such as the Karnataka B.Sc Nursing syllabus for entrance exam 2025 and the B.Sc Nursing entrance exam eligibility.

Karnataka B.Sc Nursing Entrance Exam Eligibility

  • Candidates must have passed Physics, Chemistry, Biology and English papers individually in 2nd PUC/ 10+2
  • Must have secured a minimum of 45% aggregate marks in PCB (40% for OBC/ SC/ ST)
  • Minimum age: 17 years as of December 31, 2025
  • Must be a citizen of India

B.Sc Nursing Entrance Exam 2025 Tips

Aspirants must take certain important measures to secure a strong rank in the KCET-2025 B.Sc Nursing entrance examination. These steps must be followed right from when they begin to fill in the B.Sc Nursing application form 2025 until the completion of the KCET B.Sc Nursing counselling process.

Complete Application Early

Complete your KCET B.Sc Nursing application form 2025 well in advance to avoid errors and confusion.

Follow Syllabus

Make sure your exam preparation is in line with the B.Sc Nursing entrance exam syllabus.

Take Mock Tests

Take several B.Sc Nursing mock tests to get familiar with the exam pattern.

Check Exam Center

Once the KCET 2025 B.Sc Nursing admit card is released, check your exam centre and plan on how to reach it.

Carry Admit Card

Remember to carry your KCET B.Sc Nursing entrance exam admit card on the day of the exam.

Time Management

Practice time management during mock tests to ensure you can complete all sections during the actual exam.
