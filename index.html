About
Amrita University

Amrita Online is the Online learning initiative of Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, one of India’s premier multi-campus and multi-disciplinary universities accredited with the highest NAAC grade of A++ and ranked 8th nationally by NIRF 2025. Amrita Online brings the excellence of Amrita University into a flexible and learner-driven online environment, empowering individuals across India and the world to pursue UGC-entitled and AICTE-approved undergraduate and postgraduate programs. The Amrita Online courses combine academic rigor, industry alignment, and global recognition (including WES equivalence in the USA and Canada). Learners benefit from live interactive sessions, recorded content, and a comprehensive Learning Management System that ensures continuous engagement and personalized academic support.
Amrita University online degrees are in high-demand areas such as Management, Computer Applications, Commerce, and Emerging Technologies as they have a strong focus on future-ready skills, designed for working professionals, graduates, and lifelong learners seeking to upgrade their qualifications without disrupting their careers. Every Amrita Online learner becomes part of Amrita University’s legacy of compassion-driven education, research excellence, and global impact, supported by a strong network of faculty mentors, industry collaborators, and alumni across the world.
