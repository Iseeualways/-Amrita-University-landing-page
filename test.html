FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Amrita Online programs

Is Amrita Online UGC-approved and recognized?
+

Yes, all Amrita Online programs are UGC-entitled and AICTE-approved. They hold the same academic value as on-campus degrees from Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, which is NAAC A++ accredited and ranked 8th in India by NIRF 2025. Our degrees are also WES evaluated for use in USA and Canada.

How does the online learning model work?
+

Amrita Online combines live interactive sessions on weekends with self-paced recorded content available 24/7. You'll have access to virtual labs, discussion forums, and a comprehensive Learning Management System. Exams are conducted online through a secure, proctored environment from your home.

What career support do you provide?
+

We offer comprehensive placement assistance including mentorship from industry experts, soft skills training, resume building, mock interviews, SWOT analysis, skill mapping, and internship opportunities. Our graduates report an average of 50% salary growth from enrollment to graduation.

Can I study abroad through Amrita Online?
+

Yes! We offer student exchange programs, study abroad opportunities, 3+1+X Integrated Master's pathways, and summer programs with leading universities in USA and Europe, including UC Davis, University of Barcelona, Oakland University, and more.

What are the eligibility criteria for admission?
+

For UG programs (BBA, BCA, B.Com), you need a minimum of 45% in 12th grade. For PG programs (MBA, MCA, M.Com), you need a minimum of 50% in your 10th, 12th, and undergraduate degree. International students are welcome and must provide a Certificate of Equivalence from AIU.

Are there any scholarships available?
+

We offer flexible payment options and affordable fee structures. The one-time registration fee is Rs. 500, with a semester examination fee of Rs. 2,500. For specific scholarship opportunities, please contact our admissions team for personalized guidance.
