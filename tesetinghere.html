Everything you need to know about Amrita Online

Amrita Online offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs including BBA, BCA, B.Com, MBA, MCA, and M.Com. All programs are UGC-approved and designed to meet industry standards while providing the flexibility of online learning.

Undergraduate programs (BBA, BCA, B.Com) are typically 3 years in duration, while postgraduate programs (MBA, MCA, M.Com) are 2 years. The programs follow a semester system with flexibility to complete at your own pace within the specified timeframe.
